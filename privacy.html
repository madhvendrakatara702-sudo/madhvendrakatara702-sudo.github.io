Privacy Policy

Welcome to Handwriting Pro. Your privacy is extremely important to us. This Privacy Policy document explains in detail how we handle your information when you use our website. By accessing or using our website, you agree to the terms described in this policy.

1. Information We Collect

We do not require users to create an account or provide personal information to use most of our services. The text you enter, files you upload, or data you process through our tools are handled locally within your browser. We do not store, save, or track this data on our servers.

However, if you voluntarily contact us through our contact form, we may collect limited information such as your name, email address, and message content. This information is only used to respond to your inquiry.

2. How We Use Your Information

Any information you provide is used strictly for communication purposes. We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties. Your data is only used to improve our services and respond to your questions.

3. File Processing and Security

Our website allows users to upload files such as PDFs and images. These files are processed temporarily in your browser using technologies like JavaScript. We do not upload your files to any external server, and no file data is permanently stored by us.

We take reasonable measures to ensure that your data remains secure. However, please understand that no online platform can guarantee 100% security.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We may use cookies to enhance user experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us understand how users interact with our website. These cookies do not store personal information.

You can choose to disable cookies through your browser settings at any time.

5. Google AdSense and Advertising

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies and other tracking technologies to show ads based on your interests and browsing behavior. This is known as personalized advertising.

Google’s use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your visit to this and other websites. You can opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.

6. Third-Party Services

We use several third-party tools and libraries to provide functionality, including:

  • EmailJS – for sending contact form messages
  • PDF.js – for reading PDF files
  • Tesseract.js – for image text recognition (OCR)

These services may have their own privacy policies, and we encourage users to review them.

7. Children’s Information

We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children under the age of 13. If you believe that your child has provided personal information on our website, please contact us, and we will remove such information promptly.

8. Data Protection and User Rights

You have the right to know how your data is used. Since we do not store user data, there is no personal data retained by us. If you contact us and wish to delete your message or information, you can request it at any time.

9. External Links

Our website may contain links to external websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices or content of those websites. We advise users to read the privacy policies of any third-party sites they visit.

10. Updates to This Policy

11. User Consent

By using our website, you hereby consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.

If you have any questions, suggestions, or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, you can contact us through the contact page available on our website.
